Introducing...me.

Posted by Mark Drew on code on January 25, 2007

Tagged under coldfusion

Ahh, Tim Buntel wants to find out more about us developers eh? I don’t think he knows the madness he is getting himself into! But in the spirit of the “Getting to know you” meme here goes:

  • 32 years old.
  • Living with my girlfriend (who is dammed awesome and plays with guns and guitars).
  • A car???, I live in London! Are you mad? (although there is a great site called www.streetcar.co.uk where I can hire a car for a few hours when I need one)
  • Rent a flat in Greenwich, where the Mean Time comes from.
  • Mac convert for years, probably first CF Developer to have an Intel Mac, happily codes in most environments as long as I have access to a Mac.
  • Not sure about cigars, but I did fail to give up smoking this year.
  • Addicted to Lost, 24 and Celebrity Big Brother at the moment (hey! its got Face from the A-Team!)
  • Apart from watching TV, I spend my time writing an IDE for Coldfusion called CFEclipse
  • When not doing the above, I play guitar and go to gigs, anything noisy with lots of guitars
  • I love cooking and if I wasn't playing silly buggers with computers I would be a chef
  • Music tastes vary from the Pixies to the Prodigy, Primal Scream to Pearl Jam, Primus to Portishead. Anything with a P. Apart from Senser who are just magic. Oh.. and Test-Icicles, and Arctic Monkeys... bah... too many to mention... anything with a good damn beat.
  • I am bilingual in spanish and english, I grew up in Bolivia you see? I have also lived in Germany and Spain (twice!)
  • I love London, but would also like to experience living in the USA. Thought San Francisco was great. Haven't been to New York yet (I know!) </ul>
